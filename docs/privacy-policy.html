Overview

MacroScope ("the App") is an AI-powered food and hydration tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

Locally Stored Data

  • Food photos - Stored on your device only
  • Meal logs - Stored in local SQLite database
  • Water intake logs - Stored in local SQLite database
  • Your Gemini API key - Stored locally on your device
  • Macro targets - Stored locally on your device

Data Sent to Third Parties

  • Food photos - Sent to Google Gemini API for analysis (using your personal API key)
  • Health data - Synced to Google Health Connect (with your permission)

Google Health Connect Integration

MacroScope integrates with Google Health Connect to sync:

  • Nutrition data (calories, protein, carbs, fats) - Read and Write
  • Hydration data (water intake) - Read and Write
  • Total Calories Burned - Read only (for future features)

This data is shared only with Health Connect and apps you've authorized in Health Connect settings. We do not have access to this data on any server.

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not operate any servers that store your data
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • Your Gemini API key is stored locally and used only for AI analysis

Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API - Used for food image analysis. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Google Health Connect - Used for health data sync. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Your Rights

  • Delete your data - Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Revoke Health Connect access - Go to Health Connect settings on your device
  • Control API key - You can remove or change your Gemini API key at any time

Children's Privacy

MacroScope is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated effective date.
