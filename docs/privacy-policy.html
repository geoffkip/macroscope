Summary: MacroScope stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information on any servers.

1. Information We Collect

MacroScope processes the following information locally on your device:

  • Meal photos and descriptions - Temporarily processed for AI analysis
  • Nutrition data - Calories, protein, carbs, fats logged for your meals
  • Water intake - Hydration logs you create
  • Daily macro targets - Your personalized nutrition goals

2. How Your Data is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. Your data never leaves your device except:

  • When you voluntarily sync to Google Health Connect
  • When meal images are sent to Google Gemini AI for analysis (using your own API key)

3. Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI

When you analyze a meal photo or description, it is sent to Google Gemini AI using your personal API key. This data is processed according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Health Connect

If you enable Health Connect integration, MacroScope can:

  • Write nutrition and hydration data to Health Connect
  • Read nutrition and hydration data from Health Connect

This data is shared only with apps you have explicitly connected in Health Connect settings.

USDA FoodData Central

When you search for foods, search queries are sent to the USDA FoodData Central API. This is a public U.S. government service. No personal data is transmitted.

4. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Store your data on our servers
  • Use your data for purposes other than app functionality

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual meals or water logs within the app
  • Clearing app data in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

6. Children's Privacy

MacroScope is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date.
